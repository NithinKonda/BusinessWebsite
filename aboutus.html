About

Where have we come from? What is our History?

In 2015, while working in the industry we visited different campuses for hiring drives. We realized that the students were not able to crack coding problems based on simple concepts of Data Structures & Algorithms. We did a market analysis and realized the unemployment rate stood at 80% due to substandard education and inexperienced graduates saturating the market. Hence, we felt the need to deliver quality education to the community and embarked on a mission to empower young engineering students who wanted to become better coders. READ MORE.


Why have we started Smart Interviews in the first place? What is our Mission?

There is a difference between working hard and working smart, and it makes all the difference in the long run. Here, at Smart Interviews, we provide you with the perfect blend of both hard work and smart work to help you become a well-qualified coder. It is a one-stop place for you to learn the smart way to crack coding interviews at top-notch product-based companies. READ MORE.
